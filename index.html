About KSG

Where craft meets precision engineering

Keystone Scientific Glass LLC is a Pennsylvania-based manufacturer of specialty laboratory glassware serving industrial, scientific, and biomedical clients across the United States.

We produce custom apparatus, reactor vessels, condenser systems, and precision glassware components engineered to exacting tolerances for demanding applications.

Our work draws on decades of combined expertise in scientific glassblowing and industrial glass fabrication, with a focus on filling the critical supply gap facing U.S. laboratories and research institutions.
